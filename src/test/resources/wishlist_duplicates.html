Concession Pricing

Proof of ID will be required for tickets purchased at concession price. Please ensure you bring your ID or membership card and present at the cinema on request.

Buying for Children

Children's tickets are only available for films rated G, PG, M, R13 or RP16. We are not permitted to sell children’s tickets to films until they have been rated as suitable.

Please note that you should check the rating, even when the children’s price is available. Children under 13 will not be admitted to R13 films. RP16 films are restricted to persons 16 and over, unless they are accompanied by a parent or guardian. Rating information appears on the film pages as soon as it becomes available.
